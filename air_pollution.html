    Air Pollution

  1. One of the main effect of air pollution of air pollution is global warming :-

    • Global warming is the increase in temperature of the Earth due to air pollution.
    • This effects the life forms on the earth’s surface.

  2. Another cause of air pollution is the depletion of ozone layer :-

    • Ozone layer in Stratosphere shield us from the harmful ultra-violet rays of the sun.
    • This layer is becoming thin allowing the harmful UV rays to pass through the atmosphere.

  3. Ways we can prevent ozone depletion :-

    • To put an end to CFCs and other ozone depleting gases.
    • Limit private vehicles driving.
    • Ban the dangerous nitrous oxide.

  4. Ways to reduce global warming :-

    • Stop emission of Greenhouse gases.
    • Use energy such as hydroelectricity, Solar , tidal and nuclear energy etc. which do not produce carbon di oxide
    • Conservation of forest.
